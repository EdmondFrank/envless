## What

`envless` is a thin Go CLI on top of [age](https://github.com/FiloSottile/age) + [sops](https://github.com/getsops/sops) that:

- replaces `.env` files with encrypted-in-repo `secrets/*.env.enc`
- preserves the `process.env.X` interface — language-agnostic via `execve`
- treats every team member, agent, and CI runner as a first-class identity
- ships nothing to a server, no account, no SaaS
